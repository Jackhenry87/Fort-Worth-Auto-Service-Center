Our Story

A Name River Oaks Has Trusted for Generations.

Roy Thieme built F.W.A.S.C on a simple promise: treat every car like it's your own, and every customer like a neighbor. Over 50 years later, that promise hasn't changed — and neither has our zip code.

We're a family-owned shop right here on River Oaks Blvd. No franchise. No corporate policies. Just Roy, Carol, and a team of skilled mechanics who know your name, know your car, and stand behind every job they do. When we fix it, it stays fixed.
